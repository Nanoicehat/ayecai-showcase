## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Detail page shows blurred cover background
When viewing an article, the detail page SHALL display a visible but non-obstructing background image that does not interfere with content readability. The background SHALL be clearer than before (reduced blur, increased opacity) while the content area maintains sufficient contrast.

#### Scenario: Detail page with cover
- **WHEN** user opens an article
- **THEN** page shows a lightly blurred background image at the top with moderate opacity
- **THEN** article content in the center is fully readable with adequate contrast
- **THEN** background image is clearly identifiable as a decorative element
